This site uses Google Fonts for the uniform display of fonts, which are provided by Google. The Google Fonts are locally installed. A connection to Google servers does not take place.

For more information about Google Fonts, please visit https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq and see Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en.
